sleeping

Lodging in Big Sur is tricky. There are only a few hotels, and many of them will be booked by the time you're reading this. Many of them are crazy expensive. So it goes.

We're going to try to reserve a group campsite in Big Sur in case you want to get in touch with your WILD SIDE. Let us know if you're interested in camping.

There's a much bigger selection of hotels in Carmel, in both sheer quantity and price diversity. You can also look into airbnbs and vacation rentals. Carmel is about an hour away from the wedding & reception venue; we'll have shuttles to pick you up for the (stunning!) drive.
